# Supply-chain-analysis

Business Problem:
The company is facing challenges in managing its supply chain efficiently, resulting in frequent stockouts of high-demand products, excess inventory of slow-moving items, supplier delays, and inconsistent delivery performance. These issues lead to lost sales, increased storage and transportation costs, and reduced customer satisfaction. The objective of this analysis is to identify bottlenecks across inventory management, supplier performance, and order fulfillment processes, enabling the business to optimize stock levels, improve delivery efficiency, reduce operational costs, and support data-driven supply chain decisions.
